Lasagna Recipe

Home

This classic lasagna recipe is a family favorite, featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. Perfect for a comforting dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the cooked beef. Stir in dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, half of the grated Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by one-third of the meat sauce, and sprinkle with one-third of the shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
