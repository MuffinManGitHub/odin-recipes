Burger Recipe

Home

This classic burger recipe is juicy, flavorful, and easy to make at home. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend cookout!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (optional toppings)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo (optional condiments)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Be careful not to overwork the meat.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties about 3/4 inch thick. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty to prevent it from puffing up during cooking.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes for medium doneness. If using cheese, add a slice on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or skillet for about 1 minute until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom buns. Add your favorite toppings and condiments, then top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!
