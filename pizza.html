Pizza Recipe

Home

This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classic! Delicious homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce have never been easier.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-30 slices pepperoni
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (232°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Arrange pepperoni slices evenly on top of the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle dried oregano and garlic powder over the pizza for extra flavor.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
